# Metrics Glossary

## Data center and grid

- PUE: facility power divided by IT power
- CUE: kg CO2e divided by IT kWh
- WUE: liters water divided by IT kWh
- Joules per request: facility energy normalized by request volume
- Peak coincidence factor: ratio of average load during peak hours to overall average

## Interoperability

- Telemetry coverage: fraction of required signals present and correct
- Interface drift: breaking changes across APIs, schemas, versions
- Migration impact: change in p95 latency and error after move
- Policy parity: consistent IAM, network policy, and secrets across sites

## AI evaluation

- Metric registry: versioned metric definitions with tests
- Robustness proxy: performance delta under controlled distribution shift
- Calibration: confidence alignment (log loss, ECE where applicable)
